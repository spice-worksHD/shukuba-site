職人の技が刻まれた古民家を、宿として再生。太い梁、漆喰の壁、土間の佇まい——本物の空間が、旅を記憶に変えます。食事は街の飲食店へ。朝食はセルフでゆっくり。宿は「眠り」に特化した、上質な滞在を提供します。 Each of our restored machiya townhouses bears the craftsmanship of generations. Thick timber beams, plastered walls, and earthen entryways tell stories that no modern hotel can replicate. Breakfast is a quiet, self-paced ritual. Dinner is the town itself.
